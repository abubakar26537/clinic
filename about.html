Our story

Built around the family, not the file cabinet.

Hajama & Herbal Clinic opened in 2014 with one idea: healthcare should feel like a relationship, not a transaction.

Our mission

Care that keeps its promises

We started as a single family-medicine practice above a bakery in Clifton. Twelve years later, we're a five-specialty clinic — but the promise hasn't changed: every patient gets enough time, a clear explanation, and a follow-up that actually happens.

Today our team of 12 specialists sees families across three generations, often in the same afternoon.

Our values

Genuine attention
