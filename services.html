Common questions

Frequently asked questions

Do I need a referral to see a specialist?+

No referral is required. You can book directly with any specialist, though our family medicine team is happy to help you choose the right one.

Do you accept walk-ins?+

We prioritize scheduled appointments but hold same-day slots for urgent visits each morning — call ahead to check availability.

What insurance plans do you accept?+

We work with most major insurance providers in Pakistan. Ask our front desk or our chat assistant to confirm your specific plan.

How do I get my lab results?+

Results are typically ready within 24 hours and shared by phone or through your patient portal.

Not sure which specialist you need?

Ask our assistant, or book with family medicine and we'll point you the right way.
